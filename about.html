Why Flame Web Design?

“There are three responses to a piece of design – yes, no, and WOW! Wow is the one to aim for”

Milton Glaser


Established in 2017 Flame Web Design aims to provide a profesional web solution for local and national small businesses and individuals.

The business operates from Perth, Scotland but that doesn't mean you have to be local. The beauty of web design means you can provide a service for someone both 1 mile away or 500 miles away.

Having recently completed a BSc in Computer Science I have an up to date knowledge of current practices and trends in Web design. I have experience in HTML5, CSS3, PHP, SQL, JavaScript, bootstrap layouts and Wordpress.

Coming from a customer service background I have spent many years working both face to face and over the phone with people from all walks of life so I know what customer experience should feel like and I aim to deliver a first rate service.

I work from home and don't host any finished websites at present so my overheads are low, this enables me to offer a cheaper service than some larger web design companies whilst having more time to focus on your design.

We are on the lookout for new and existing businesses to increase your online presence and drive new business to you. Why not get in touch to see if we can provide that spark to ignite your business.
