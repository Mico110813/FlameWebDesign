Bespoke Design


Every website and webpage needs to look the part in the modern age. I will work with you to make sure that every part of the website is to your liking, changing images, text, placement is all part of the journey.

Its no secret that mobile browsing is taking over from more traditional forms of web browsing so all of our sites will be fully responsive, allowing for more people to view your content without straining their eyes or giving up completely. Try viewing this site on mobile or shrinking your browser to see it in action

Keeping you updated every step of the way and working to your own specifications I will provide you with a custom built website to be proud of. If you like the setup and design of an existing website then let me know and I can recreate something similar for your own business or personal use

I aim to have most websites completed within a couple of weeks, sometimes longer, sometimes quicker but I will never deliver something half ready or that you are not happy with.

If its just a one page shop front or a multi page website you require then I'm sure we can work together to get your business online for the modern market, contact me outlining your requirements and i'll get back to you ASAP.


Image Sourcing


Not everyone is going to have images to hand when thinking about content for their chosen design. If you need to add some images or graphics I can search the web on your behalf to utilise some free for commercial use images and incorporate them into your new website.

This has the benefit of both freeing up your precious time whilst giving your site a better appearance. The only thing I ask is to provide your own logo/Business name as this is not something I can do

If you already have an existing site that you are happy with but need some fresh images incorporating that's not a problem either and I would be happy to help you with this.

Image sourcing will be included within any quote that requires it and itemised seperately.


Going live


Unfortunately at the present time I don't offer any webhosting service. This is mostly down to time management and being able to keep my costs down, passing on the savings to yourself. This has the benefit of giving you freedom to shop around for the best deal and reducing web design costs. I use 1 & 1 as I found them cost friendly and reliable.

Once you have a provider and your chosen domain name, all I would need is a few details and I will get your site up and running when it is completed. I'll handle all the file transfers and making sure it all works so you don't have to.

Obviously I wouldn't just upload a site you aren't happy with so a final version of the site will be available for you to look through before it goes live


Aftersales

Don't worry, I'll contact you after a few weeks of having your site up and running and if anything needs slightly changing or amending its not a problem

I'll be in touch after a few weeks just to ensure that your site is everything you need it to be and have been happy with the service you've received. I might even ask if its ok to use your site in my portfolio which helps me promote my own business in turn

If you need me any sooner then please by all means contact me and I'll get back you as soon as I possibly can.
